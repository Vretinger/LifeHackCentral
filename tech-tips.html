Password Management

Use a password manager to securely store and generate complex passwords for your accounts. This eliminates the need to remember multiple passwords and helps improve security by using unique passwords for each account.

Screen Time Management

Set limits on your screen time to reduce distractions and improve productivity. Use built-in features on your devices or third-party apps to track and manage your screen time, and schedule regular breaks to rest your eyes and reduce digital fatigue.

Backup Your Data

Regularly backup your important files and data to an external hard drive, cloud storage, or a dedicated backup service. This protects your data against loss due to hardware failure, theft, or other unforeseen events.

Stay Updated

Keep your devices and apps up to date with the latest software updates and security patches. This helps ensure optimal performance, compatibility with new features, and protection against security vulnerabilities.

Keyboard Customization

Customize your keyboard layout and shortcuts to better suit your preferences and workflow. You can remap keys, create custom shortcuts for frequently used phrases or emojis, and even change the keyboard theme or layout for a more personalized experience.
