Declutter hack

Declutter with the Four-Box Method: When organizing a room, use four boxes labeled "Keep," "Donate," "Trash," and "Store." Sort items into these boxes to quickly clear out clutter and decide what to do with each item.

Use Drawer Dividers

Maximize drawer space and keep items organized by using drawer dividers or small containers to separate different categories of items, such as socks, underwear, or office supplies.

Double-Up Hangers

Save space in your closet by using soda can tabs to create double hangers. Simply hook one hanger through the hole of the soda tab to hang another garment beneath it.

Label Everything

Invest in a label maker or use printable labels to label containers, shelves, and drawers. Clear labeling makes it easy to find items quickly and ensures that everything has a designated place.

Repurpose Magazine Holders

Use magazine holders to store items vertically on shelves or in cabinets. They're great for organizing cutting boards, baking sheets, Tupperware lids, and more.
